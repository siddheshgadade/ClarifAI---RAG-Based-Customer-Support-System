TROUBLESHOOTING
==================================================

SmartWatch Pro X1 Issues:

Problem: Watch won't turn on
Solution: 
- Charge the watch for at least 30 minutes
- Press and hold the power button for 10 seconds to force restart
- If issue persists, contact support

Problem: GPS not working accurately
Solution:
- Ensure location services are enabled on your phone
- Update the watch firmware to the latest version
- Perform GPS calibration in Settings > GPS > Calibrate
- Use the watch outdoors with clear sky view for best results

Problem: Battery draining quickly
Solution:
- Disable always-on display in Settings
- Reduce screen brightness
- Turn off unused sensors (GPS, heart rate monitoring when not needed)
- Check for app updates that may improve battery efficiency

Wireless Earbuds Elite Issues:

Problem: Earbuds won't pair
Solution:
- Place earbuds in case and close lid for 10 seconds
- Remove earbuds and they should enter pairing mode automatically
- On your device, forget the earbuds from Bluetooth settings
- Re-pair by selecting "Earbuds Elite" from available devices
- If still not working, perform factory reset (see manual)

Problem: Poor sound quality or connection drops
Solution:
- Ensure earbuds are within 30 feet of your device
- Remove any obstacles between earbuds and device
- Clean the earbud mesh with a soft, dry cloth
- Update firmware through the TechGear app
- Reset the earbuds to factory settings

Problem: One earbud not working
Solution:
- Place both earbuds in the case for 30 seconds
- Remove and check if both are working
- Clean the charging contacts on both earbuds and case
- Perform a factory reset if issue persists

Q: How do I update the firmware on my SmartWatch Pro X1?
A: Download the TechGear app on your smartphone, connect your watch via Bluetooth, and navigate to Settings > Device > Firmware Update. Follow the on-screen instructions.

Q: Are your products covered by warranty if purchased from third-party retailers?
A: Yes, as long as the retailer is an authorized TechGear dealer. Purchases from unauthorized sellers may not be covered.

Q: Can I use my SmartWatch Pro X1 while swimming?
A: Yes, the watch is water-resistant up to 50 meters and can be used for swimming. However, it's not suitable for diving or high-pressure water activities.

Q: How do I clean my Wireless Earbuds Elite?
A: Use a soft, dry, lint-free cloth to wipe the earbuds. For the mesh, use a dry cotton swab gently. Never use liquids or cleaning solutions.
